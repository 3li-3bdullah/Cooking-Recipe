# Cooking-Recipes
Cooking Recipes shows you most recipes ,
so you will able to do your favorite food by your self :) .

## Packages i am using:
- http: ^0.13.4
- get: ^4.5.1
- webview_flutter: ^3.0.0
- flutter_native_splash: ^1.3.2

## Technologies the app demonstrates:
- GetX as State management.
- MVVM architecture.
- Binding.
- API.
